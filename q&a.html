Q&A

Q1: How do I book tickets online?

A: Booking tickets online is easy! Simply go to our website, select the movie, date, time, and the number of tickets you want then proceed to checkout to complete your purchase.

Q2: Can I choose my seats when booking tickets online?

A: Yes, you can! Our website allows you to select your preferred seats from an interactive seating map, giving you the flexibility to choose the best available seats for your movie-going experience.

Q3: What payment methods do you accept for online ticket bookings?

A: We accept a variety of payment methods, including credit/debit cards,and online banking. You can choose the payment method that is most convenient for you during the checkout process.

Q4: Is it possible to cancel or change my booking after I've purchased the tickets online?

A: Yes, you can cancel or change your booking, but please note that our cancellation and change policies may vary depending on the movie, date, and time of the screening. We recommend checking our terms and conditions or contacting our customer support for assistance with cancellations or changes.

Q5: Do I need to create an account to book tickets online?

A: While creating an account is not mandatory, we highly recommend it! Creating an account allows you to save your preferences, view your booking history and receive updates on upcoming movies and promotions. It also makes the booking process faster and more convenient for future purchases.
